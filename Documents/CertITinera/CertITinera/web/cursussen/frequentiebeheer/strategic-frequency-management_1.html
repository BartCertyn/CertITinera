Strategic Frequency Management

Objectives

Frequencies are a very limited national resource. Therefore management of these resources is crucial to avoid interferences, as well on a national, regional and worldwide basis. Standardisation is also necessary to ensure industry-wide interoperability.

During this 1/2 day course

  • You will get a good understanding of the national/international bodies involved, and how they interact
  • You will acquire a good knowledge of the national/international allocation tables and how you have to interprete them
  • You will know the differences of allocation, allotment and assignment
  • future tendencies will be revealed (IMT, SRD,…) in relation to the World Radio Conference 15

It is adressed to

  • Wireless Strategic Teams/Engeneers/Managers
  • Wireless consultants
  • Strategic Telecom Managers/Teams

Prerequisites

  • You have to know basic principles as frequencies, bandwidth, modulation and multiplexing,...
